Clean Beauty Since 1996

100 Years of Women Inspired by the Sea By Jenefer Palmer, Founder

OSEA was founded three decades ago, but our story began over a century ago. My grandmother Elsa was a great believer in the power of the sea. When she was 44 years old, Elsa had a terrible fall, injuring her leg and leaving her bedridden. One night, a message came to her in a dream: The ocean will heal you. The next morning, she asked her husband to take her to the Long Island Sound and carry her into the water. She returned again the next day. And the next. Elsa swam in the frigid waters every day for weeks, and the dream became a prophecy: Her leg healed. When I had a family of my own, we moved to Southern California, never straying far from the ocean. At the time, almost everything in the skincare world seemed to be made with artificial fragrances, dyes and synthetic ingredients, and none of it worked very well. I knew I could do better. Borrowing from my grandmother’s wisdom, I collected seaweed and started mixing my own formulas. That is how OSEA was born. Today it’s called clean beauty, but in our family, it is what it has always been: a simple idea that the ocean should be part of everyone’s life—and the conviction that it already is.



Skincare From the Sea

Since day one, our family has put our values into every product we make and into everything we do. OSEA stands for ocean, sun, earth and atmosphere. These are the natural elements of wellness and a promise that every decision, every product is made with consideration of our impact on our planet and our collective well being.

Click for more

We believe that animal rights matter, which is why we have always used vegan and cruelty-free ingredients. All OSEA products are vegan-certified by PETA and Cruelty Free-certified by Leaping Bunny International.

At OSEA, we’ve always been guided by the belief that sustainability is our only future. That’s why when we launched our business back in the 90s, we chose to package in glass and prioritize naturally-derived ingredients and sustainable packaging materials. In 2020, we took it one step further by becoming a Climate Neutral certified and Ocean Positive verified brand.

In 2002, we became the first company to sign The Compact for Global Production of Safe Health and Beauty Products, an initiative from the Environmental Working Group. Their mission is to ensure products are “free of chemicals that are known or strongly suspected of causing cancer, mutation or birth defects.” We continue to lobby our legislatures and advocate for safe skincare for all.
